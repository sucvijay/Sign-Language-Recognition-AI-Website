The Importance of Sign Language

Sign language is a vital tool for communication for those who are deaf or hard of hearing. It allows them to express themselves and interact with others effectively.

By providing a means to translate sign language into text, we can bridge communication gaps and promote inclusivity.

Sign Language Detection Website

Imagine a website that can detect sign language gestures and translate them into text in real-time. Such a website could empower deaf or hard of hearing individuals to communicate more easily with others who may not understand sign language.

By using machine learning algorithms and computer vision techniques, this website could analyze hand movements captured by a camera and convert them into corresponding text, opening up new possibilities for inclusive communication.
